Mobile App Developer

2020-10-11

The healthy drink recognition mobile phone application is an Andriod system-based recognition software. After taking a picture of the mainstream soda coke with the App, the backend system can recognize whether the drink is Diet Coke, or Coke Zero, or Classic Coke.

The Baidu EasyDL customized artificial intelligence platform is first used for the App to train a deep learning model that can recognize different types of drinks and then connect to the App’s framework. The drink’s picture will be uploaded to the app’s backend to obtain data before further analysis and processing. Finally, the results will be displayed on the interface. The types of drinks currently recognized by the App include Diet Coke, Coke Zero, and Classic Coke (brands which are owned by the Coca-Cola Company), with an accuracy rate of over 95%.
