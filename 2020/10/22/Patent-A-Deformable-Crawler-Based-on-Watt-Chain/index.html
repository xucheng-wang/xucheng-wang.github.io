Patent: A Deformable Crawler Based on Watt Chain

2020-10-22

1.Description of program

A deformable crawler based on Watt’s linkages, advancing through caterpillar crawling and rolling motions, and featuring environmental adaptability, mobility, as well as multi-terrain movement.

Prototype

2.Patent information:

Patent approved by National Intellectual Property Administration, PRC

3.Structural features

  1. A deformable crawler based on Watt’s linkages has two motions: caterpillar crawling and rolling.
  2. For obstacle crossing, it can adjust the contacting angle of the caterpillar and obstacles by deforming to increase stability.
  3. For ditch crossing, it can add the size of ditch crossing by deforming.
  4. In the case of muddy skidding and other caterpillar failures, it can roll only by deforming.
  5. Diversion can be realized by the velocity difference between left and right chains.
  6. Caterpillar motion and rolling motion can be conducted simultaneously to increase the maximum moving speed.
